Bank Balance

User Guide

Startup

Launch Bank Balance

Setup

If this is your first time launching Bank Balance you will be taken to the account settings screen.


Select either AIB or BOI to setup your account details.


You can further protect your security by turning on PIN protection from this screen and setting a four-digit pass-code.

Setup Bank Account(s)

When you select either AIB or BOI in the previous step you will be presented with the appropriate individual bank account settings screen.


You need to fill out all the information that you have supplied to your bank when setting up your online banking account.


Note: If you haven’t registered some information with your bank (for example: the last four-digits of your Visa number with AIB) then you can safely ignore entering those details.


It is very important that you fill out the details in this screen with care, as entering incorrect details here could lock you out of your online banking account.


After you have setup your bank accounts you should click ‘Done’ on upper left hand portion of the screen to display your current balances.

PIN Protection

If you have turned on PIN Protection, you will be asked to enter your four-digit numeric pass-code each time the application is launched. This helps protect your privacy and security when sharing your phone with someone.


Bank Balance

After a few moments, your current bank balances for your online accounts are shown.
